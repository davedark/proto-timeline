Contacting the person

We'll send the person confirming your identity an email. You must contact them before they receive it.

You need to ask someone who:

  • have known you personally for at least 2 years, for example as a friend
    or neighbour
  • work in or be retired from a recognised profession
  • have a current UK passport

Make sure the person knows your date of birth, including the year.
